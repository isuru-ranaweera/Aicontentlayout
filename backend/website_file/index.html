Ergonomics in food preparation exploring robotic solutions for commercial kitchens

RoboAI Academy project on robotic automation for Pori central kitchen

On 23.1.2026 a student team from the RoboAI Academy investigated ergonomic solutions for food preparation at the City of Pori's central kitchen by analyzing robotic automation for transferring cooked food. The simulation results indicated that full automation was impractical due to cost and speed, suggesting improvements like refined kettle mechanisms and assistive devices instead. These outcomes provide a reference for the city to evaluate future ergonomic and automation improvements in its kitchen facilities.
